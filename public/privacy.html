Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2024

1. Introduction

Space Clicker ("we", "our", "the app") is a simple web-based game. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information.

2. Data We Collect (With Your Consent)

We only collect data after you explicitly start the game. The data includes:

  • Device Information: Browser type, screen size, language, timezone
  • IP Address: Your network IP address (automatically collected by web servers)
  • Location (GPS): Your precise location only if you grant permission
  • Camera Access: Camera feed only if you grant permission (used for optional features)
  • Game Score: Your game score and player name (if provided)

3. Why We Collect This Data

We collect this information to:

  • Provide game functionality and features
  • Improve the gaming experience
  • Analyze usage patterns and performance
  • Display your location and score on leaderboards (if you consent)

4. Your Control & Consent

You are always in control:

  • All camera and location access requires your explicit browser permission
  • You can deny camera/location access and still play the game
  • You can revoke permissions anytime in your browser settings
  • Data collection only starts after you click "Start Game"

5. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We do not automatically delete data, but you can request deletion by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption to protect your data during transmission.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at: sailendra126@gmail.com
